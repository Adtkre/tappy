How it works

01 — LAPTOP

Open Tappy on your laptop

Run the desktop app. It shows your local IP and waits quietly for a phone to connect, nothing happens until you allow it.

02 — PHONE

Open the app and tap connect

Tappy finds nearby laptops on your Wi-Fi automatically, or you enter the IP shown on screen.

03 — CONTROL

Move, click, and type remotely

Your phone becomes a trackpad and keyboard. Close the laptop window any time to disconnect instantly.

TAPPY WORKS OVER YOUR LOCAL NETWORK ONLY
